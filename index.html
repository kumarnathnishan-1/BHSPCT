About Our Parishad

The Bangladesh Hindu Students Parishad Chawkbazar Thana stands as a beacon of unity, spirituality, and cultural preservation for the Sanatana Dharma community in Chattogram.

Our mission is to foster a sense of brotherhood, uphold our divine traditions, and provide a platform for collective worship, learning, and social service. Through our various events, festivals, and spiritual gatherings, we strive to nurture the younger generation with the timeless wisdom of our ancestors.

  • Spiritual Harmony & Unity
  • Social Welfare & Support
  • Vedic Education & Cultural Events
